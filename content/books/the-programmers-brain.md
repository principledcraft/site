This book looks at the cognitive science behind programming: how your brain reads, understands, and remembers code. Drawing on research into memory, attention, and learning, it explains why some code is harder to work with than others and offers practical techniques for reducing cognitive load. It connects what we know about how brains work with the everyday experience of reading and writing code.
