A classic guide to becoming a better developer, updated for its 20th anniversary. It covers personal responsibility, career development, coding techniques, design principles, and project management. The book is built around practical advice rather than theory, encouraging developers to think critically and keep adapting. Many of its ideas have since become standard practice.
